Section 2.C.2.250

Value Description German Interpretation Comment Chapter
BIO Biological
The dangers associated with normal biological materials. I.e. potential risk of unknown infections. Routine biological materials from living subjects. 2.C.2.250
COR Corrosive
Material is corrosive and may cause severe injury to skin, mucous membranes and eyes. Avoid any unprotected contact. 2.C.2.250
ESC Escape Risk
The entity is at risk for escaping from containment or control. 2.C.2.250
AGG Aggressive
A danger that can be associated with certain living subjects, including humans. 2.C.2.250
IFL MaterialDangerInflammable
Material is highly inflammable and in certain mixtures (with air) may lead to explosions. Keep away from fire, sparks and excessive heat. 2.C.2.250
EXP Explosive
Material is an explosive mixture. Keep away from fire, sparks, and heat. 2.C.2.250
INF MaterialDangerInfectious
Material known to be infectious with human pathogenic microorganisms. Those who handle this material must take precautions for their protection. 2.C.2.250
BHZ Biohazard
Material contains microorganisms that are an environmental hazard. Must be handled with special care. 2.C.2.250
INJ Injury Hazard
Material is solid and sharp (e.g., cannulas.) Dispose in hard container. 2.C.2.250
POI Poison
Material is poisonous to humans and/or animals. Special care must be taken to avoid incorporation, even of small amounts. 2.C.2.250
RAD Radioactive
Material is a source for ionizing radiation and must be handled with special care to avoid injury of those who handle it and to avoid environmental hazards. 2.C.2.250
