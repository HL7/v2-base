Introduction

The chapters 1, 6 (IN2, IN3, UB1 and UB2), 7 - 12 are left out so far.

The final standard is still kept as a collection of WinWord Documents. Though other (better) formats (SGML/HTML/DB) are discussed the actual one contains some inconsistencies listed below.
