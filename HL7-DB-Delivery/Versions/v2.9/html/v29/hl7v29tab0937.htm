MCS Sub-type of RSLT (Result) This term identifies observations that give overall culture outcome, when no specific organism is being named.


MID Sub-type of RSLT (Result) This term identifies any other observations about the isolate.


MIG Sub-type of RSLT (Result) This term identifies growth levels of an isolate – can be numeric or categorical.


MIN Sub-type of RSLT (Result) This term identifies observations that name a specific organism either at the species or the genus level.

MOD Sub-type of RSLT (Result) This term identifies observations that do not fit the culture status, but is not at the isolate level.


MSS Sub-type of RSLT (Result) This term identifies observations on gram stains (and other stains or smears) on the clinical sample.


SUP Supplemental Result
